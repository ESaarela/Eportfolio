About me

My name is Eetu Saarela, I am a Finnish software developer. I am 17 years old, and I live with my parents and my sister in the pöykkölä district of Rovaniemi. I am currently studying software development in the Jokiväylä unit of the Lapland Education Centre REDU.

My free time hobbies include, but are not limited to: playing video games, riding on my moped, spending time with my friends and going to the gym.

Motivation is the driving force behind my actions. I have an inherent desire to succeed, not just for personal gain, but also to make a meaningful impact on the projects and endeavors I undertake. I continuously seek inspiration from my goals, mentors, and the people around me. I am motivated by the opportunity to contribute to something greater than myself, to create positive change, and to leave a lasting impression.
